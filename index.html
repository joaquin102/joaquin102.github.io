The Social Network that is actually social

Your School or University in one app

  • Download on the App Store

SCHOOLS AND UNVIERSITIES Find your classroom. Your classmates are already there

Wouldn’t it be great to have your entire School or University in one app? No need to add each one of your classmates, just join the classroom and start being social. You can chat and interact with them, share posts and even invite them to events or meetups.

  • Classrooms All your classmates in one place. No hassle of asking their phone number or Facebook account or email address to be able to talk to them. Just join your classroom and you’re all set!
  • Make friends Have access to anyone on your entire School or University. You can chat with any user and add them to your contact list. Making new friends and being actively social is now easier than ever!
  • Meetup Want people to join you for your soccer game, beach day, art gallery or any plan? Easily set meetups with anyone on your School or University. Make plans and have them join you or just join someone else’s!

Features us Chatroom with all your friends

All your classmates and contacts in one place. See who’s online and start chatting! You can also send voicenotes, sketches, pictures and videos or even share your location. Looking for someone new to chat with? Use the search tool and make new friends!

GROUPS Make friends with the same interests as yours

Making new friends has never been this easy! Join or make groups of people with the same interests, tastes or hobbies as you. Share posts inside your groups, chat with your new friends and make plans together. Be social, it’s fun!

  • Groups Make groups with your best friends, family, football team, dancing class or any community. Venner is like having a social network for the people you share with or for making new friends. Create a group of something you want to belong to, and invite people or wait for them to get to you!
  • Meetups Now that you belong to many groups, start making plans with them! Movies, hiking, bbq, adventures, fun night out, you name it. Just start a meetup and have others join you!
  • Interact See what your friends are up to in the Feed section and interact with them. Share pictures, videos and links. Check if you have any meetups coming up or go to the Chatroom to see if anyone’s online. Interaction has never been so fun!
  1. Search Venner makes it easy for you to find your School or University
  2. Select Select your classroom. Search for your School Year or University Courses
  3. Classmates Take a look at who your classmates are
  4. Join Send a request to join your classroom. Wait for your classmates to accept you and start enjoying Venner!
